1.1 Upgrading from Windows XP to Windows Vista:  Leave Xbox 360 Wireless Gaming Receiver plugged in

When upgrading from Windows XP to Windows Vista, leave the Xbox 360 Wireless Gaming Receiver plugged in.

If the receiver is not plugged in during the upgrade, you may need to reinstall the software for your Xbox 360 Wireless Gaming Receiver.
